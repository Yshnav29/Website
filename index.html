Feed your loved ones

World has changed lot. It is the world of self sufficient people. Be suffiecient by yourself without anyone's help. This robotic arm can feed you. It will be the best gift you can present to your relatives if they are unable to eat food by themselves.

Specifications

  • 3 degrees of freedom (Two 20kg/cm torque servo motors and a 1.8kg/cm torque servo motor.)
  • Ultrasonic sensor to measure distance for the safety purpose of the user.
  • Mobile application for controlling motion and customization of motor angles.
  • Rechargeable
  • Battery level indicator

Design and components

  • 3D printed material
  • Desined in fusion 360
  • ESP32
  • Servo motor
  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • 12v lead acid battery
